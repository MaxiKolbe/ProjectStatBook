This section contains the **core gameplay systems and mechanical references within the [Cyberpunk 2021](./Cyberpunk_2021/) universe**.

These pages serve as a **gameplay reference archive**, documenting the rules, mechanics, and systems used to represent abilities, cyberware, and other elements tied to the broader story.

## About

The Cyberpunk 2021 gameplay archive highlights the systems that govern how characters operate within the setting. These include mechanics related to cyberware, skills, and other technological or physical enhancements.

Character statistics and skills are **loosely based on the tabletop RPG *Cyberpunk 2020*** by R. Talsorian Games.  

The system provides a framework for representing character abilities, but it is used primarily as a **narrative reference rather than strict gameplay mechanics**.
