This section contains character sheets, factions, abilities, and worldbuilding related to the **Cosmere TTRPG**.

## About

The Cosmere TTRPG is an ongoing archive of characters, powers, and lore inspired by Brandon Sanderson’s **Cosmere universe**.  
This section documents major figures, magical systems, organizations, and narrative elements across the many worlds of the Cosmere.

Character abilities and mechanics are **loosely inspired by the Cosmere Roleplaying Game and the magical systems present in the novels**, including Allomancy, Surgebinding, Awakening, and other Invested Arts.

The system serves primarily as a **narrative and worldbuilding reference**, tracking characters, powers, and relationships rather than enforcing strict gameplay mechanics.
