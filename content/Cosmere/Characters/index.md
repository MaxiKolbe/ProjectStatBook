# Characters

This section contains a collection of the **more prominent characters within the [Cosmere TTRPG](./Cosmere/) universe**.

These pages serve as a **character reference archive**, documenting major individuals involved in the events, factions, and conflicts across the many worlds of the Cosmere. Each entry includes background information, abilities, and relevant details tied to the broader narrative.

## About

The Cosmere character archive highlights key figures who play important roles within the interconnected worlds of the Cosmere. These characters may include warriors, scholars, worldhoppers, political leaders, and individuals touched by the power of Investiture.

Many of these figures possess access to the various **Invested Arts**, such as Allomancy, Surgebinding, Awakening, and other magical systems unique to their respective worlds.

Character abilities and powers are **loosely inspired by the Cosmere Roleplaying Game and the magic systems depicted in Brandon Sanderson’s novels**.

The system provides a framework for representing character abilities, but it is used primarily as a **narrative reference rather than strict gameplay mechanics**.
