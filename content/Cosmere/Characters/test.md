# Spren Talents

- **Test Assistance (▷ or ↻, 1 Focus)**  
  Grants advantage on tests related to this spren's knowledge and philosophy.

- **Covert Scouting (2 Focus)**  
  Stealthily scout an area within spren bond range.

- **Oath Encouragement (▷ or ↻, 2 Focus)**  
  Gain advantage on your next test to face self doubt, fear, or your obstacle.

- **Sudden Warning (3 Focus)**  
  Alert you to imminent danger.

- **Translation (2, 2 Focus)**  
  Translate spoken and signed Rosharan language for one minute.

# Stormlight Actions

- **Enhance (1)**  
  Spend 1 Investiture to gain **Strength +1** and **Speed +1** until the end of your next turn.

- **Breathe Stormlight (2)**  
  Draw Stormlight from infused spheres within 5 ft and recover Investiture.

- **Regenerate (▷)**  
  Spend 1 Investiture to recover **1d6 + your current tier Health**.
